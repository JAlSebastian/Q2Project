Pink Sand Beach, Zamboanga City

Zamboanga is the city where I was born. Even though people stereotype Zamboanga as a dangerous place because of the wars that happened in the past. It still treasures a lot of beuatiful places and scenery. In Zamboanga, you get to experience eating seafood by the beach, snorkeling and of coure their beautiful pink sand beach.
Camp Netanya, Batangas

This is my first out of town trip with my family. This place is inspired by Santorini in Greeece. When I was here, I experience a lot of activies such as kayaking, swimming with the fishes and island hopping. You can also take IG worthy photos with the Greek vibes of the place.
Batanes

It is known for its remote location which is situated at the top of the Philippines. In fact to arrive in Batanes, you need to board a plane which is smaller than a regular one. In Batanes, you get to experience breathtaking views, their beutiful culture and their hospitble locals.
